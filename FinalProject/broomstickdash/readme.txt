# CIT190 Final Project: Wizards Arcade

-------------------------------------------
## Game Information
-------------------------------------------
**Broomstick Dash**: Take flight on your enchanted broomstick and navigate through challenging obstacles using the arrow keys on your keyboard.
------ Game Purpose: 
            The game starts with your broomstick positioned at the bottom of the screen. Obstacles will appear randomly on the screen.
            Fly your broomstick through gaps and openings in the obstacles to continue flying.
            The game ends if your broomstick collides with an obstacle or the edge of the screen.Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control the movement of the broomstick. 
            Avoid obstacles by maneuvering your broomstick up or down.
------ Game Controls:
            Up Arrow: Move the broomstick upward
            Down Arrow: Move the broomstick downward
            Right Arrow: Speed up the broomstick
            Left Arrow: Slow down the broomstick
------ Future Release:
            Allow the user to save high score and add different level of difficulty.
